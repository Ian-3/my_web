The Zodiacs



Capricorn

As a capricorn, you have an amazing work-ethic. You're dedicated to whats assigned to you. However, this can make you seem very uptight to other people. You speak your mind, which is a blessing and a curse. You aren't necessarily selfish, but you put yourself first. If helping others doesn't get in the way of what you're doing, then you'll hapily help. Just like an earth sign, you're as stubborn as a rock.





Aquarius

As an aqaurius, you are very independent and you enjoy it, sometimes too much. You care a lot about being different and standing out. This can lead to people thinking of you as selfish, but you are a nice person. You're a little bit of an odd ball, but who isn't, right? Like any air sign, you're a free spirit.





Pisces

As a pisces, you're very emotional. You attach to your emotions easily, this makes you very true to yourself and others. You value honesty. You're very good at handling other people's emotions and helping when others are emotionally distressed. You do, however, have a tedency to let your emotions take over. Overall you are a nice person. Just like a water sign, your emotions tend to shift.





Aries

As an aries, you're a firey person. That's right, rumors are true, you've got anger issues. But that's ok, your anger is fueled by your drive. You're a natural leader and tends to do well with communicating your thoughts. While your anger tends to slip, you know that you truly care for what you're talking about. Just as a fire sign is, you're passionate.





Taurus

As a taurus, you are a kind person. You want to be a good person, and enjoy seeing others happy. However, this isn't your fuel, as you highly value your alone time. This can lead to a little bit of laziness. You care for others to an extent. You expect kindness back, and when it isn't reciprocated, you become a stone cold person. As an earth sign should be, you're stubborn.





Gemini

As a gemini, you're very intellignet and articulate. Your communication skills are unmatched. You have the natural ability to eaily read people, making it very easy for you to empathize with others. This also makes you good at manipulation. This can be a blessing or a curse, depending on the gemini in question. Just like any air sign, you're a free spirit.





Cancer

As a cancer, you have a kind soul. You enjoy being kind and have a soft exterior. However, you aren't happy-go-lucky, you have a mean side and it tends to easily slip out when you feel emotional. You can go from a friendly to hostile mood in an instant. At times, you can lose track of yourself and act more selfish than usual, though you don't mean to. As a water sign, your emotions tend to shift.





Leo

As a leo, you're very selfish. While yes, many people stereotype leos, you're just looking out for yourself first. Your priorities tend to be mostly centered around yourself. You have a hard time being empathetic towards other people and overall struggle to pick up on others emotions. However, you are one of the most confident signs. You know yourself most and don't let others walk all over you. Like a fire sign is, you're very passionate.





Virgo

As a virgo, you enjoy organization. Even if you aren't necessarily a neat freak, you always tend to have an organized way of doing things. You're intelligent and a hard worker. Similar to capricorn, you have a strong work ethic. However, while you aren't necessarily emotional, you aren't emotionally distant either. You tend to have more empathy than people give you credit for. Just like any earth sign, you're stubborn.





Libra

As a libra, you're a very chill person. You relax easily and are one of the most overall stable signs. However, your relaxation can quickly turn into laziness. You struggle to start and stay working for long periods. You're an introvert at heart, and while you aren't necessarily shy or anxious, you highly value your alone time. As an air sign, you're a free spirit.





Scorpio

As a scorpio, you are a big loner. This isn't to say you don't have friends, but when it comes down to it, you like being alone. You have a little bit of a mean side, but you know that and you own it. You're a very precise person, you know what you want and you're gonna get it. While you aren't the most emotionally-smart person, you're very intelligent in other areas. Just like a water sign, your emotions tend to shift.





Sagittarius

As a sagittarius, you're very adventurous. You love to explore and are always searching for something new. You're a kind person, and love to help others. However, you're also independent, and don't rely on anyone. You tend to be very emotionally-dumb, and while you have a lot of empathy, you have a hard time dealing with emotional people and understanding them. As a fire sign, you're very passionate.
